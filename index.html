Policy spec Business

Date rules — the most frequent source of bugs
Real case: a BRD stated "coverage = date-to-date − 1 day, 23:59:59" and also allowed invoices to be backdated 5 days. The two rules interact — if the invoice is backdated, when does coverage start? The BRD never answered it.
Age rules — most commonly left blank
These two answers apply to every plan. The actual min / max age numbers are set per plan below — in the VFS BRD, Plans 1 and 2 allowed 1–75 while Plans 3 and 4 allowed 15–75. When "age range" is filled with a dash "–", that is not "no limit" — it is unanswered.
Policy duplication rule
One real BRD said "1 transaction can have multiple insured? Yes" but also "Min & Max insured per transaction: 1". Those two sentences contradict. Here there is exactly one place to answer it.
