Policy spec Business

Date rules — the most frequent source of bugs
Real case: a BRD stated "coverage = date-to-date − 1 day, 23:59:59" and also allowed invoices to be backdated 5 days. The two rules interact — if the invoice is backdated, when does coverage start? The BRD never answered it.
Age range — most commonly left blank
When "age range" is filled with a dash "–", that is not "no limit" — it is unanswered. Two ShopeePay VN bugs (off-by-one + wrong reference date) came from exactly this gap.
Policy duplication rule
One real BRD said "1 transaction can have multiple insured? Yes" but also "Min & Max insured per transaction: 1". Those two sentences contradict. Here there is exactly one place to answer it.

Premium & pricing Business

Premium is rarely a single flat number. Pick the method that matches the BRD, then run the simulator with a sample value — that is what catches the classic range-boundary bug (does an item priced exactly at a range edge fall in the lower or upper range?).

Amartha charges "1.5% of loan". Without a floor, a tiny loan yields a premium of a few rupiah that the provider will reject at settlement. Always ask whether a floor/cap exists.
